*   **Mining Stocks as "Leverage"**: Mining stocks act like a mirror that amplifies Bitcoin's price fluctuations. If Bitcoin drops by 1%, their profit margins are slashed by far more. Investing in them is essentially akin to "investing in Bitcoin with leverage."
*   **The Report Card Called "Earnings"**: Giants like IREN (Iris Energy) and CleanSpark released figures that fell short of market expectations. The needle of reality has finally pierced the balloon of inflated expectations.
*   **"Prey" for Short Sellers**: The market is cold-blooded and never misses a weakened target. "Vultures" seeking short-term profits have noticed the fading profitability of mining companies and are hitting them with heavy sell orders.

To put it in plain terms: they are the "geese that lay golden eggs," but when the feed (Bitcoin price) decreases, the health of the geese themselves deteriorates rapidly. This extreme volatility is the true nature of mining stocks.
